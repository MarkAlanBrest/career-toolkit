Find a Job – Online Job Search Resources

Major Job Boards

Indeed

One of the largest job search websites with millions of listings.

Tip: Search by job title and location. Upload your resume so employers can find you.
LinkedIn Jobs

Professional networking site with job listings and employer connections.

Tip: Create a strong LinkedIn profile and connect with employers in your field.
ZipRecruiter

Job board used by many employers across the country.

Tip: Use job alerts so new positions are emailed to you automatically.
Glassdoor

Job listings plus company reviews and salary information.

Tip: Research companies before applying to learn about work culture and pay.

Trade and Skilled Labor Jobs

ConstructionJobs.com

Jobs focused on construction and skilled trades.

Tip: Search by trade such as welding, HVAC, electrical, or carpentry.
Roadtechs

Industrial, mechanical, and traveling trade jobs.

Tip: Many positions involve travel and can offer higher pay.
iHireConstruction

Construction industry job listings.

Tip: Upload your resume so employers can contact you directly.

Government Jobs

USAJobs

The official job site for federal government positions.

Tip: Federal applications can take longer, but benefits are often excellent.
GovernmentJobs.com

State and local government job listings.

Tip: Search for city, county, and state employment opportunities.

Local Job Listings

The Vindicator Job Listings

Local job advertisements from the Youngstown area.

Tip: Many small local employers still advertise in local newspapers.
Local Indeed Jobs

Example search for welding jobs in the Youngstown area.

Tip: Replace the job title in your search to find positions in your trade.
