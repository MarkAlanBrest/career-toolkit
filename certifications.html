Professional Career Certifications

New Castle School of Trades offers short career certifications designed to help students strengthen their skills and improve their employability in the skilled trades and related industries.

These certifications are designed to demonstrate professional knowledge in areas such as workplace safety, industry best practices, and career readiness skills. Certificates may be completed online and can be included on resumes to show employers a commitment to professional development.

Examples of Certifications

The following are examples of the types of certifications that may be available through Career Services and professional development programs.

  • Workplace Safety Awareness
  • Lockout / Tagout Safety Training
  • Jobsite Hazard Recognition
  • Professional Communication in the Workplace
  • Career Readiness Certification
  • Job Interview Preparation
  • Construction Workplace Safety
  • Trade Industry Professionalism

Why Certifications Matter

Earning certifications helps students demonstrate additional skills and knowledge beyond their primary training program. Employers often value candidates who take initiative to expand their knowledge and maintain strong professional standards.

These certifications can be added to resumes, cover letters, and job applications to help graduates stand out when applying for employment opportunities.
